Nurturing a business idea or a seed-stage startup/SME to a full-fledged enterprise requires more than just skills; it demands the right environment, support, and resources. The FGN-ALAT Digital Hubs, located in strategic states across the country, serve as a crucible for innovation. Here, active and aspiring entrepreneurs can refine their business models, gain access to resources, network with industry experts, and collaborate with peers. The incubation hubs ensure startups and SMEs have the foundation they need to scale and thrive in today's competitive market.

In the diverse ecosystem of entrepreneurship, not every business owner is drawn solely to technology. Recognizing this, the FGN-ALAT Digital Skillnovation Program has also designed specialized SME Bootcamps for youth entrepreneurs who are powered by passion, creativity, and inherent business acumen. These bootcamps offer an immersive experience into the world of business for businesses in traditional sectors that are not heavily dependent on technology.
