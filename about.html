About This Program

Beyond mere skills acquisition

What you will learn

In today's fast-evolving world, the potential of our youth remains one of our greatest assets, and nurturing this potential is at the heart of the FGN-ALAT Digital Skillnovation Program. This holistic initiative, birthed from a strategic partnership between Wema Bank and the Federal Government of Nigeria, through the Office of the Vice President, aims to equip our young minds with a versatile set of skills and opportunities tailored to their individual aspirations and market realities.

Beyond mere skills acquisition, the program is set to create a ripple effect of socioeconomic benefits across the nation. By empowering our youth, we are not just fostering individual successes; we are investing in the broader narrative of national progress, promoting job creation, boosting innovation, and paving the way for a more prosperous future. With each participant we train, mentor, or incubate, we are making a conscious investment in the nation's future, ensuring that Nigeria remains at the forefront of the global digital economy.

Furthermore, this program underscores Wema Bank's commitment to driving positive change and sustainable development in Nigeria. It's a testament to our belief in the boundless potential of the Nigerian youth. By creating a platform that seamlessly blends training, mentorship, and practical entrepreneurial experiences, we are building bridges to a future where every young Nigerian has the tools, knowledge, and support to make their mark in the world. In addition to empowering young entrepreneurs, the program also caters to career-driven professionals, offering them tailored pathways to sharpen their skills and navigate the complexities of the modern, digital-oriented workplace with confidence and competence.

At its core, the FGN-ALAT Digital Skillnovation Program is more than just a training initiative – it's a comprehensive ecosystem designed to foster innovation, growth, and success, shaping the next generation of Nigerian entrepreneurs and professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the Program Start

The program is set to kick off on Monday, November 27, 2023, and we have a packed schedule for you.

What do I Expect in the Program

• Virtual Training: The program will begin with virtual training sessions designed to equip you with in-demand tech skills, soft skills, and business management skills, depending on your chosen track or category.

• Hands-on Physical Training: You will also have the opportunity to experience hands-on physical training at our state-of-the-art digital hubs spread across the country.

• Mentorship: Those who complete the virtual and physical training will also be able to have one-on-one mentorship opportunity with top professionals and business leaders from Nigeria and around the world.

• Networking: You can connect with fellow participants and industry leaders to build valuable relationships.

• Internship Placements: If you are a jobseeker, you can explore internship opportunities to gain practical experience and further enhance your skills in a real-world work setting.

• Access to Market: Active and aspiring business owners taking part in the program can also showcase their products and services to their target audience, through our e-commerce platform and exhibition slots in major international and local trade fairs and exhibitions.

• Access to Finance: Entrepreneurs can tap into various funding opportunities to unlock the growth and scalability of their businesses.

How do I Track-Specific Benefits

• Entrepreneurs (Business owners and aspiring business owners): Business incubation, access to funding (grants, equity investment, and soft loans), business mentorship, and access to market opportunities.

• Working-Class Professionals: Employability training, career leadership training, industry-specific career mentorship, and networking opportunities.

• Job Seekers: Internship placements, employability training, career mentorship, and networking opportunities
